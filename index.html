Mission statement

Our goal is NOT to destroy any relationship you may have with your sibling. Instead, it's meant to strengthen it through good old fashioned pestering. When's the last time your brother left your door open a crack? Or your sister took your stuff without asking? Or your sibling let the dog poop in your room instead of theirs (or taking the dog out, come on)? Are you gonna let that slide? No! That's why you're here: to learn the best way to push all your siblings' buttons. Be you an older sibling, younger sibling, or twin, this should be your go to resource to bug your best friend by birth - with love of course.

about the author

Elijah Holt (striped shirt) is a proud older brother to Jack Holt (graduation gown). They are exactly two and a half years apart. Eli says there's a three year different and Jack says two. As kids, they were the same size, inseparable, and often mistaken for twins - much to Eli's consternation. They were tennis partners in high school and now both go to school in California. The Holts grew up in Seattle, WA, so neither are native to the areas around their schools. Eli goes to Berkeley, and as of the Fall 2020, Jack is an incoming freshman at UCLA. Eli makes a joke out of the two schools being rivals, but he is very proud of his brother, and wishes him the best for his freshman year.

Eli's family and friends have provided him with pictures of their lovely sibling relationships that he hopes he can destabilize just a little bit through this comprehensive guide to being your most annoying self.
